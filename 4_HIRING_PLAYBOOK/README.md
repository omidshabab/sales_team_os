Title: Hiring Playbook
Owner role: Recruiter
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: End-to-end hiring process for sales roles with SOPs and templates.
How to use (1-2 lines): Follow steps for requisition, sourcing, screening, interviewing, and offers.

1. Requisition approval — **Recruiter** (30m): confirm role, budget, JD, and timeline.
2. Sourcing — **Recruiter** (4h): post JD, outreach to talent pools, referrals.
3. Screening — **Recruiter** (45m per candidate): resume filter + phone screen.
4. Role-play — **Hiring Manager** (60m): use `/role_play_scenarios/*`.
5. Panel interview — **Hiring Manager** (60m): structured questions (competency, situational).
6. Reference checks — **Recruiter** (60m): 2–3 references.
7. Offer & negotiation — **Recruiter** (45m): use `/8_TEMPLATES/offer_letter.md`.
8. Onboarding handoff — **Recruiter** (30m): coordinate with `/6_ONBOARDING/`.
