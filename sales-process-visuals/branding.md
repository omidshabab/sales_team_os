Title: Branding Guidelines — Sales Visuals
Owner role: RevOps Lead
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Ensure visual consistency across sales diagrams and infographics.
How to use (1-2 lines): Apply these colors and typography when updating or creating visuals.

- Colors:
  - Primary: #1E88E5 (blue)
  - Secondary: #43A047 (green)
  - Accent: #FB8C00 (orange)
  - Neutral: #455A64 (slate), #ECEFF1 (light)
- Typography:
  - Title: system-ui, 22–28px, semibold
  - Body: system-ui, 14–16px, regular
- Spacing:
  - Grid: 8px base unit
  - Margins: 16–24px outer, 8–12px inner
- Accessibility:
  - Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for text
  - Include `<title>` and `<desc>` in SVG; `aria-label` in HTML
