Title: Proposal & Negotiation SOP
Owner role: AE
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Standardize proposals and deal terms; control discounts.
How to use (1-2 lines): Use matrix and steps; secure legal/finance approvals where needed.

1. Draft proposal — **AE** (20m): use `/8_TEMPLATES/proposal_snippet.md`.
2. Pricing & terms — **AE** (15m): align with discount matrix `/7_SOPS/discount_matrix.csv`.
3. Stakeholder review — **AE** (10m): confirm decision process and constraints.
4. Legal/Finance sign-off — **Head of Sales** (30m): approve non-standard terms.
5. Negotiation call — **AE** (30m): T-give/T-get; timebound; mutual action plan.
6. Finalize & send — **AE** (10m): e-sign; handoff schedule.
