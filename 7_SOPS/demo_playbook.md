Title: Demo Playbook
Owner role: AE
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Deliver a crisp 20-minute demo that converts to proposals.
How to use (1-2 lines): Follow pre-checklist, agenda, and post-demo steps for consistency.

Pre-checklist — **AE** (10m)
1. Confirm goals and roles
2. Tailor demo script and data
3. Prepare success story and proof points

Agenda (20m) — **AE**
1. Intro & objectives (2m)
2. High-level value (3m)
3. Core workflows (10m)
4. ROI and success stories (3m)
5. Next steps & timeline (2m)

Post-demo — **AE** (5m)
1. Send summary and materials
2. Book proposal call
3. Update CRM fields
