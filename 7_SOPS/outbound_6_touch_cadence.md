Title: Outbound 6-Touch Cadence SOP
Owner role: SDR
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Create meetings via a structured 12-day cadence.
How to use (1-2 lines): Follow exact timing and templates; log each touch in CRM.

Cadence (12 days)
1. Day 1 — **SDR** (5m): Email #1 + LinkedIn connect (use `/8_TEMPLATES/cold_email_1.md`).
2. Day 3 — **SDR** (5m): Email #2 follow-up + profile-based value prop.
3. Day 5 — **SDR** (5m): Call #1; voicemail script; LinkedIn message.
4. Day 7 — **SDR** (5m): Email #3; share case study snippet; ask for 20-min.
5. Day 9 — **SDR** (5m): Call #2; objection handling; propose time slots.
6. Day 12 — **SDR** (5m): Breakup email; invite to resources; meeting link.
