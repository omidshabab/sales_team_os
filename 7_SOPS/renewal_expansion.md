Title: Renewal & Expansion Playbook
Owner role: CSM
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Drive retention and expansion via structured QBRs and signals.
How to use (1-2 lines): Follow cadence; log signals; coordinate with Sales on upsell.

1. Onboarding outcomes — **CSM** (30m): confirm value realization milestones.
2. Health monitoring — **CSM** (30m/month): adoption, NPS, usage trends.
3. QBRs — **CSM** (60m/quarter): goals, outcomes, roadmap alignment.
4. Expansion signals — **CSM** (ongoing): users, teams, new use cases.
5. Renewal motion — **AE+CSM** (30–60m): pricing, term, value recap.
