Title: Lead Capture & Qualification SOP
Owner role: SDR
Last updated: 2025-12-25
Purpose: Standardize inbound/outbound lead capture and ensure fast qualification.
How to use (1-2 lines): Execute steps for every new lead; log in CRM; escalate qualified leads.

Inbound
1. Triage new lead within 15 minutes — **SDR** (15m): check source, intent, completeness.
2. Qualify using BANT-lite — **SDR** (10m): Budget, Authority, Need, Timing indicators.
3. Book meeting — **SDR** (10m): send Calendly; confirm agenda; log activity.
4. Assign owner — **RevOps** (5m): route per territory; notify AE.

Outbound
1. Build list — **SDR** (60m): ICP filters; verify emails; enrich LinkedIn.
2. Run 6-touch cadence — **SDR** (30m/day): see `/7_SOPS/outbound_6_touch_cadence.md`.
3. Qualify replies — **SDR** (10m per reply): use discovery script short.
4. Handoff SQLs — **SDR** (5m): convert lead; assign AE; book meeting.
